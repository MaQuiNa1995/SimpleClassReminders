# SimpleClassReminders

**SimpleClassReminders** is an addon that provides on-screen reminders for important class basics, helping players avoid forgetting essential buffs, pets, and weapon enhancements.

## How It Works

The addon continuously checks your character and group state and displays clear text reminders at the top-center of the screen whenever something important is missing.

Examples:
- A group member dies and loses a buff
- You forget to resummon a pet
- A weapon enchant or poison is missing

## Current Features

### General
- **Healthstone Reminder**
  Shows a warning if you are a Warlock, or if there is a Warlock in your group or raid, and you do not have a Healthstone.

### Pets
- **Pet Reminder**
  Alerts you if your class/spec should have a pet and no pet is currently summoned.

### Buff Reminders
- **Power Word: Fortitude (Priest)**
  Warns when Fortitude is missing on one or more alive group or raid members.
- **Mark of the Wild (Druid)**
  Alerts when Mark of the Wild is missing on one or more alive group or raid members.
- **Blessing of Bronze (Evoker)**
  Warns when Blessing of Bronze is missing on one or more alive group or raid members.
- **Arcane Intellect (Mage)**
  Warns when Arcane Intellect is missing on one or more alive group or raid members.
- **Devotion Aura (Paladin)**
  Notifies when Devotion Aura is not active.
- **Skyfury (Shaman)**
  Notifies when Skyfury is missing on one or more alive group or raid members.

### Weapon and Class Utilities
- **Shaman Weapon Enchant Reminder**
  Alerts Shamans when their weapons are missing temporary enchantments.
- **Paladin Rite Reminder**
  Alerts Paladins when the Rite weapon enchant is missing.
- **Death Knight Runeforge Reminder**
  Alerts Death Knights when their weapon is missing a Runeforge enchantment.
- **Rogue Poison Reminder**
  Warns Rogues when either a lethal or non-lethal poison is not applied.

## Localization

Currently supported languages:
- English
- Spanish

If your WoW client is set to another language, the addon defaults to English.

## Smart Behavior

Reminders automatically hide when they are no longer relevant, including:
- All required buffs are active
- The player is dead or a ghost
- The player is mounted
- The player is using a vehicle

Additional features are planned, including:
